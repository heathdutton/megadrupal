-- INSTALLATION --

1 - Install as any normal drupal module;

2 - Then go to the configuration page for the module, on the module page or on
the configuration menu: config/services/get_social;

3 - Module will show you your mail and site name, click on activate account,
wait some seconds and the page should automatically reload;

4 - After the installation you will see a button that will take you to
GetSocial website in order to configure your social apps;
