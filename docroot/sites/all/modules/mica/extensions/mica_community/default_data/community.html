Communication with researchers is crucial and stimulating. Mica offers out of the box different communication means such as discussion groups (forums), news and events announcement, and blogs.

News

This section is to announce important news.

Events

Forums

This section is an online discussion board where people can hold conversations in the form of posted messages.

Blogs

This section is for blogs i.e. a series of posts that are time stamped and are typically viewed by date as you would view a journal.
