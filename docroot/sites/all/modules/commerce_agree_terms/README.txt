Commerce Agree to Terms
=======================

This module provides a commerce checkout pane with an
'I agree to the Terms and Conditions' checkbox linked 
to your custom Terms and Conditions page.

Use
===

Enable the module as usual
Go to Store > Configuration > Checkout Settings
Position and configure the Terms and Conditions pane
