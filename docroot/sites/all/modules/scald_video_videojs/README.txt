Supply VideoJS player for Scald_video module.

Install:
  - Activate it
  - Download videojs library: http://www.videojs.com/ and copy it in /sites/all/libraries

Configure:
  - Configure a context to use the new video player provides, on admin/structure/scald/video/contexts
