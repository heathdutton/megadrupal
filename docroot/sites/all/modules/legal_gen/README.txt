-- SUMMARY --

Legal Generator allows an administrator to generate a site's:

- Terms of Service (TOS)
- Privacy Policy

For a full description of the module, visit the project page:
  http://drupal.org/project/legal_gen


-- REQUIREMENTS --

None.


-- INSTALLATION --

* Install as usual, see http://drupal.org/node/70151 for further information.


-- CONFIGURATION --

* Navigate to Administration > System > Legal Generator.

* Enter the Company and Site Info to use in the TOS and Privacy Policy.

* Click "Save Settings".

* Navigate to the "Terms of Service" tab to view the TOS content.

* Navigate to the "Privacy Policy" tab to view the Privacy Policy content.
