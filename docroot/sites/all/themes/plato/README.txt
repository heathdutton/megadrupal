### Sasson - advanced drupal theming.

## Plato was made for OpenIdeal
OpenideaL is an idea management system (IDMS), adapted for companies & 
organizations interested in sharing strategic decisions and future planning of
products and services with their customers. 
OpenideaL is an open source project based on Drupal.

## What is it
Plato is a Drupal 7 theme. It was created as part of the OpenIdeal project but 
realesed as a standalone theme as well.
